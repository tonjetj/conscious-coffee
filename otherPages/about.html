Why organic?

It's better for you!

Organic coffee has more nutrients than non-organic. Most people don't get enough of potassium, niacin or magnesium in their diet. Though the amount is small, any extra sources of these vital minerals are helpful. Organic coffee also contains vitamin B1, B2, B3 and B5.

It's better for the farmers health and their economy!

No pesticides means no danger in inhaling around the crops. Organic growing methods lets the farmer avoid working with poisons and pesticides.

Because organic coffee is of higher quality, the coffee farmers can charge more money for their product - which means that they can pay their workers a better wage.

It's better for the communities and the planet!

Cleaner beans = cleaner land, water and air.

Organic farming is more eco-friendly in that it emits less carbon than chemical farming. Organic coffee is grown the natural way - in the shady, lush forests that provide homes and sustenance for indigenous plants, animals, and peoples.
